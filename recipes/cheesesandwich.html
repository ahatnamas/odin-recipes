Cheese Sandwich

Description

A beautiful way to make the world's easiest sandwich!


You Will Need:

  • Cheese
  • 2 x bread
  • Butter

Method:

  1. Start by taking two slices of bread and spreading the butter evenly on one side of each slice.
  2. Add the cheese to the middle of one buttered slice.
  3. Top with the second slice to close the sandwich.
  4. Cut in half and enjoy.
